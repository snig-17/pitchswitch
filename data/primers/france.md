# France

## History
Two-time World Cup winners (1998, 2018) and a tournament powerhouse. Les Bleus combine elite individual talent with tactical discipline.

## Key Players
Kylian Mbappe, the explosive forward whose pace terrifies defenses, is the focal point. Deep squad strength across every position.

## Tournament Context
Consistent deep runs in major tournaments; reached the 2022 final. Pragmatic in tight games, devastating in transition. Rarely beaten without a fight.

## Style
Solid defensive block that springs lightning counter-attacks. Mbappe in space is one of the most dangerous sights in world football.
