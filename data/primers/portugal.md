# Portugal

## History
European champions in 2016, Portugal are a golden-generation side with attacking flair and steely resolve.

## Key Players
Cristiano Ronaldo, the record-breaking goalscorer and relentless competitor, anchors a squad rich in creative midfielders and pacey wingers.

## Tournament Context
Always dangerous in knockout football; capable of beating anyone on their day. Strong from set pieces given aerial threats.

## Style
Possession-based with quick wide overloads. Lethal when crosses reach the box; Ronaldo's movement keeps defenders honest.
