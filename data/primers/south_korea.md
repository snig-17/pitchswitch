# South Korea

## History
Asia's most consistent World Cup participant, famously reaching the semi-finals as co-hosts in 2002. The Taegeuk Warriors are renowned for relentless energy and never-say-die spirit.

## Key Players
Son Heung-min, a world-class forward and captain, drives the attack with pace, finishing, and leadership. A hard-working collective backs him up.

## Tournament Context
Often underestimated by broadcasters but capable of stunning bigger nations, as they have repeatedly proven. Thrive as underdogs with nothing to lose.

## Style
Tireless pressing and rapid counter-attacks. Danger spikes when Son carries in transition; they punish complacent opponents in the final minutes.
