# Russia

## History
Hosts of the 2018 World Cup, where they overachieved by reaching the quarter-finals and knocking out Spain on penalties.

## Key Players
A hard-running, physically robust squad that maximizes collective organization. Set-piece deliveries are a recurring weapon.

## Tournament Context
Capable of riding home support and tournament momentum. Defensively stubborn and willing to take games to penalties.

## Style
Compact defending and direct, energetic attacks. Danger builds from set pieces and second balls around the box.
