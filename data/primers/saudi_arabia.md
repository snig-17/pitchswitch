# Saudi Arabia

## History
A regional powerhouse in Asian football and a regular World Cup qualifier. The Green Falcons stunned the world by beating Argentina at the 2022 World Cup.

## Key Players
A cohesive, well-drilled squad built on a strong domestic league. Disciplined defending and quick transitions over individual stardom.

## Tournament Context
Frequently dismissed by broadcasters, yet capable of famous upsets. Compact, organized, and dangerous on the break against favourites.

## Style
Deep defensive shape with sudden vertical counters. Danger arrives quickly when they win the ball and break with numbers.
