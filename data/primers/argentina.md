# Argentina

## History
One of football's giants, Argentina have won the World Cup three times (1978, 1986, 2022). La Albiceleste are perennial contenders and fierce rivals of Brazil in South American football.

## Key Players
Lionel Messi, the talismanic captain and one of the greatest players of all time, leads the attack. Strong supporting cast in midfield and a resilient defensive core.

## Tournament Context
Reigning world champions after lifting the trophy in 2022. Always carry the weight of expectation; thrive in knockout pressure. Set-piece threat and Messi's free-kick danger make them lethal in tight games.

## Style
Patient build-up that explodes into quick combinations in the final third. Dangerous on the counter when opponents commit forward.
