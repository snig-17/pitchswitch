# Germany

## History
Four-time World Cup winners (1954, 1974, 1990, 2014) and one of the most successful nations in the sport's history. Die Mannschaft are synonymous with tournament efficiency.

## Key Players
A blend of experienced leaders and a new generation of technical midfielders. Strong in organization and dead-ball situations.

## Tournament Context
Historically peak in the latter stages, though recent campaigns have been turbulent. Always a threat when their press clicks.

## Style
High pressing and quick vertical passing. Build danger through midfield control and aggressive ball recovery in advanced areas.
