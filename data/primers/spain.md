# Spain

## History
World champions in 2010 and back-to-back European champions (2008, 2012). La Roja defined an era with tiki-taka possession football.

## Key Players
A midfield engine of technically gifted passers controls the tempo. Emphasis on youth development keeps the pipeline of talent flowing.

## Tournament Context
Dominate the ball and grind opponents down. Can struggle to break low blocks, but relentless circulation creates late chances.

## Style
Short passing, positional play, and patient probing. Danger builds through sustained final-third possession rather than direct attacks.
