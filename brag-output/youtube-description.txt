PitchSwitch — the AI that cuts you to the goal BEFORE it happens ⚽️

You picked a game. It's nil–nil, going nowhere... and the other match just erupted. In the World Cup group stage, both games in a group kick off at the exact same minute — and you can only watch one screen. Pick wrong and you miss the moment that flips the entire group.

PitchSwitch fixes that. It watches every simultaneous match at once, gives each one a live "danger" score from the play-by-play (balls into the final third, pressure in the box, shots, corners, penalties), and — crucially — tracks how fast that danger is RISING. The moment one match is about to boil over, the Director cuts your feed to it and shows you exactly WHY: an on-screen danger bar for each game and the differential that triggered the switch.

On our test matches it flagged goals with about 70 seconds of warning on average. So you're already watching the right game when the net ripples.

Like NFL RedZone — but for soccer, and it gets you there before the goal instead of after.

Under the hood, three IBM technologies:
• Granite — calls every switch out loud in a line a real TV presenter would say, grounded in team context
• Docling — parses the team primers and the Laws of the Game that ground the narration and the "Coach" explainer
• Watson Text to Speech — the spoken British-commentator voice

It also leans the switching toward YOUR teams — including the small nations broadcasters ignore.

Built for World Cup 2026.
